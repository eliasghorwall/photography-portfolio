About.

My name is Elias and I created this portfolio during an internship at Atelier Disko in Hamburg. The idea was to build something to showcase my photographs in a very focused and distraction-free manner. Thanks to their great guidance I learned a ton of new things along the way about the conceptual and technical work that goes into such a project. I had a lot of fun and hopefully you enjoy the results. If you have any questions feel free to ask.

Breathtaking landscapes.

270318 — Canada

This earth is gorgeous, but I feel we sometimes tend to forget that. I created this series to remind us of and showcase the incredible landscapes Canada has to offer. I was able to spend three weeks there during March of this year. Specifiaclly, I visited Nova Scotia. Have fun.

Under the mist.

130901 — Hamburg

It doesn’t happen very often, but when it does I always feel the need to go out and shoot. This is a collection of misty pictures I captured during the time. I don’t know about you, but I think mist has something very mysterious to it and it can transforms places completely. It’s magic.

Hongkong at night.

220515 — Hongkong

I travelled to Bangkok this year and the most fascinating thing in there, for me at least, were the nights. All the little shops, the lights and signs make this a wonderful place to take stunning photographs in. Enjoy.
